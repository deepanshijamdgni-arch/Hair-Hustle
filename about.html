The Story

Born from a Crown's Struggle

"I spent years searching for products that actually understood my hair. So I created them myself."

Hair Hustle was born in 2019 in a small apartment kitchen, where founder Naomi Clarke began experimenting with raw botanical oils, butters, and ancient hair care traditions passed down from her grandmother.


What started as a personal solution to chronic dryness, breakage, and a damaged scalp grew into something far bigger ‚Äî a brand that speaks to every woman who's ever been told her hair is "too much" or "too different." Hair Hustle is the answer: luxurious, intentional, and deeply nourishing.
