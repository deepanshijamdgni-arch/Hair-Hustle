Do you ship internationally? +

Yes! We ship to 40+ countries worldwide. International shipping typically takes 7–14 business days. Free shipping on orders over $75.

Are your products safe for colour-treated hair? +

Absolutely. All Hair Hustle products are sulfate-free and colour-safe. They're formulated to nourish and protect without stripping colour.
